## Wandelbots

[Wandelbots](https://www.wandelbots.com) is a software-centric platform for industrial robot automation. Its core is a unified, vendor-agnostic operating system and cloud infrastructure that helps teams plan, simulate, deploy, and optimize robotics workflows across hardware brands and environments. The platform brings modern software practices -- real-time control, simulation, APIs, and cloud-native tooling -- to automation and robot orchestration.
